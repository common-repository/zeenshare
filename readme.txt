ZeenShare integration with wordpress. Access ZeenShare workspace directly from your website.

== Description ==

ZeenShare lies on a mere idea : make your life easier. That's why our portal enables you to annotate 
your collaborative documents whether it is a text or not : 
you can upload .ppt extensions as well as pdf documents of images.

ZeenShare is a portal to save time and reduce bulk e-mails. Use it to collaborate with your co-woworkers or your client.

Zeenshare wordpress plugin adds to your site the opportunity to registered accounts to put documents directly in 
your zeenshare domain, without having to login again.

== Installation ==

You need a Zeenshare Pro domain to activate the plugin. If you still don't have domain, you can create a zeenshare : [https://zeenshare.com/fr/team_join](https://zeenshare.com/fr/team_join).

1. Upload `zeenshare` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure the plugin in 'Settings> zeenshare' menu in wordpress
You private Key is available on your domain settings page on zeenshare. 
You domain name is the subdomain of the full domain used to access to zeenshare. 
For exemple, if you full domain is netsas.zeenshare.com, you domain name will be netsas.
